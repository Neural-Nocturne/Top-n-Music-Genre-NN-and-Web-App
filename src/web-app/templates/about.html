    Neural Nocturne is a project by Elvin Carmona, Mark Jordan and Michael Levins. It is a music genre classification Neural Network implemented with a multi-layered perceptron (MTP). It has a testing accuracy of 93%, and can detect 10 different musical genres: blues, classical, country, disco, hiphop, jazz, metal, pop, reggae and rock. It can accept Youtube links to music videos, and can also accept .mp4 and .wav audio files. We hope you enjoy Neural Nocturne!
